Dive into the world of Roblox and turn from a player into a developer! In these two workshops, you will learn how to create your own 3D worlds using Roblox Studio.
We will explore how to build obstacle courses (Obbys), move objects, and take our first steps with the Lua programming language. The two sessions complement each other, but can also be booked individually. If you already have some experience, you can jump straight into Part 2; for beginners, we recommend starting with Part 1.
Our mentors will be there to help you bring your ideas to life. Just bring your creativity!

What you need:
A laptop for programming. If you don't have one, book an additional computer product or write to us. We'll find a solution!
Paper and pens for your designs and notes.

If you are under 13 years old: One:n legal guardian to accompany you during the dojo. The guardian also needs a separate ticket. If the combination of tickets your family needs is no longer available, just write us and we'll see if we can find a solution.

Accessibility: All areas of the library in Schöneweide are accessible to people with limited mobility. There is no audio description system. Some doors can be difficult to open.
We generally offer our workshops in German (spoken and written). Almost all mentors also speak English.
If you need support in other languages (e.g., Russian, Arabic) or additional assistance (e.g., screen readers), please let us know. We strive to make our workshops accessible to everyone.

You are also welcome to bring something small to eat/drink. We always try to provide a small buffet.
